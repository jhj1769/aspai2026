Welcome to Pohang, South Korea!

We are delighted to invite you to Pohang, Republic of Korea, for the 2nd Asia-Pacific Symposium on Process and Artificial Intelligence (ASPAI 2026).

The symposium will be held from August 24 to August 28, 2026, at the POSCO International Center, POSTECH (Pohang University of Science and Technology).

The Asia-Pacific Process Intelligence (APPI) Network invites researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to this forum dedicated to advancing process intelligence, AI-driven process analytics, and context-sensitive BPM across the Asia-Pacific region.

APPI Network was established in 2025 to address the region's unique challenges‚Äîdiverse regulatory environments, varying BPM maturity levels, and heterogeneous digital ecosystems‚Äîwhile leveraging opportunities enabled by AI, automation, and process data.

This year's symposium offers a rich program: an Industry Day in Seoul, a two-day symposium in Pohang with keynote speakers and paper presentations, and a Process Mining Summer School.

We look forward to meeting you in Pohang!
