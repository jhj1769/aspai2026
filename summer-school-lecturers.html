Summer School Lecturers

The APPI Summer School brings together distinguished academics and industry experts from around the world to deliver a comprehensive curriculum on process mining and AI.

Academic Lecturers

Prof. Wil van der Aalst

RWTH Aachen University, Germany

Topic: Foundations of Process Mining

Known as the "Godfather of Process Mining," Prof. van der Aalst is a pioneer in the field of process mining and business process management. He is the author of the seminal book "Process Mining: Data Science in Action."

Prof. Marlon Dumas

University of Tartu, Estonia

Topic: Business Process Management & Conformance

Prof. Dumas is a leading researcher in business process management, known for his work on process modeling, conformance checking, and his widely-used textbook on BPM.

Prof. Hajo Reijers

Utrecht University, Netherlands

Topic: Process Analytics & Optimization

Prof. Reijers is an expert in process analytics and redesign, with extensive research on improving business processes using data-driven approaches.

Prof. Minseok Song

POSTECH, South Korea

Topic: Object-Centric Process Mining

Prof. Song is a leading researcher in process mining in the Asia-Pacific region, with expertise in object-centric process mining and healthcare process analytics.

Industry Speakers

Industry Expert (Celonis)

Celonis

Topic: Hands-on Workshop: Enterprise Process Mining

A Celonis expert will lead the hands-on workshop on enterprise-grade process mining, covering real-world applications and best practices.

Industry Expert (SAP)

SAP

Topic: Process Mining in Enterprise Systems

An SAP expert will share insights on integrating process mining with enterprise resource planning systems and real-world implementation challenges.

What You'll Learn

  • Foundational concepts and algorithms in process mining
  • Practical skills using industry-standard tools (PM4Py, Celonis)
  • Advanced topics including OCPM and predictive monitoring
  • Real-world case studies and industry applications
  • Networking opportunities with experts and peers
